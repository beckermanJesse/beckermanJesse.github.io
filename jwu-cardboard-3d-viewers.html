JWU Cardboard
3D Viewers

Collaborated with the professor in charge of Johnson & Wales University's Innovation Lab in altering and re-designing the Google Cardboard into a compact, 3D viewer for JWU Admissions. The package would be sent to prospective students of JWU that are interested in attending but cannot visit for a tour of the campus. The package would include the 3D Viewer, admissions papers, and instructions for assembly of the 3D viewer.
